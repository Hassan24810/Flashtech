Our Story

Flash Tech was born from a simple frustration: too many apps, too many notifications, too much chaos. Our founders, Alex and Sarah, were drowning in messages across WhatsApp, Slack, email, and a dozen other platforms. They knew there had to be a better way. What started as a weekend project to unify their own messaging quickly grew into something bigger. Today, Flash Tech helps over 50,000 professionals take control of their conversations and reclaim their focus.
